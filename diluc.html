Diluc

Appearance: Story:

Diluc is a tall and slender man with pale skin, red eyes and bright red hair, worn in a low ponytail that extends to his mid-back. His expression usually makes him appear stern, exasperated or unimpressed.

Diluc wears a black dress shirt, a long white vest, and a black ascot with a red gem. Over this he dons a dark brown coat with golden trimmings and tassels, ending with black fur-trims. He wears black pants which are embellished with chains on his right thigh, while his Vision, attached to a belt, is settled upon his left thigh. Diluc also wears cuffed, dark brown boots held by multiple buckles, and a pair of black gloves which he has a habit of adjusting. Their palm sides are all red while the backs are black with a matching red diamond pattern. His outfit, Darknight Blaze, is described to be a smart and tasteful suit fit for a nobleman.



As the city of ballads and wines, Mondstadt's alcohol industry is renowned all over Teyvat.
As the owner of the Dawn Winery, Diluc is essentially in charge of the industry. This means he has a huge stream of revenue and an entire network of information in the form of tavern patrons' gossip right at his fingertips.
In a way, he is the uncrowned king of Mondstadt.
